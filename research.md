## Research

My PhD research was focussed on large scale mammogram image analysis and the synthesis of medical image and text information with the aim of identifying tumours as well as estimating cancer risk. Previously, I worked towards breast cancer molecular subtype classification from ultrasount images while persuing my master’s degree at Tsinghua University, my introduction to machine learning with medical data.


As my undergraduate studies were focussed on physiology, including clinical drug research, the core motivation for my studies in computer vision and machine learning is to further understanding of human physiology and pathology.
I enjoy and am open to sharing and discussing research at the intersection of healthcare and technology and hope to share more posts and invite discussion in the future.
