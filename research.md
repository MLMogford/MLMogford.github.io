## Research

My PhD research is focussed on data handling and machine learning within the BRAIx project which is pioneering AI applied to large-scale breast cancer screening with broad aims including risk measurement, model explainability and multimodal information fusion to improve screening outcomes and reduce harms and stress for women. I am contributing to the exceptional and interdisciplinary BRAIx team by applying research and novel techniques to data handling and multimodal fusion, leveraging my background in biomedical and computer science.
